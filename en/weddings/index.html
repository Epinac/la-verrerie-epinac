Frequently asked

Before you write.

What is the maximum capacity?

120 standing cocktail guests in the grounds, 60 seated diners in the state rooms. Beyond, we study case by case (marquee in the grounds).

Can we bring our own caterer?

Yes. We work with a list of recommended local caterers, but you remain free to choose your own. The professional kitchen is at your disposal.

How late can we celebrate?

Amplified music must end at 2 am, but the bar continues. The estate stands isolated from any dwelling, allowing a true night of celebration.

How many can sleep on site?

Six manor rooms welcome your closest family. [exact capacity TBC] Other guests can stay in partner accommodations in Épinac and Autun.

Is the estate accessible?

The ground floor and grounds are accessible. [upper-floor accessibility, accessible bathrooms TBC]

Do you accept religious ceremonies?

Yes, in the grounds or indoors as you wish. Several places of worship are nearby (Épinac, Autun) for outside ceremonies.

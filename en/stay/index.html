Folio I · The Rooms The Rooms

Rare volumes, original parquet.

Six metres of ceiling height. A herringbone parquet that creaks underfoot. The window opens onto the nine thousand four hundred square metres of grounds, and the silence that follows.

The rooms have kept their original volume — they have kept their light too, the kind of late-November afternoon light that gilds the panelling.
